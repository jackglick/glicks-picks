Every pitch tells a story

The model reads three years of Statcast data to predict what a pitcher will do tonight.

01

Crunch the pitches

Every pitch from 2022–2024 — velocity, movement, location, outcome. That's the raw material for 14 custom features per start.

02

Know the matchup

Who's batting? How often do they swing and miss? What's the park factor? Who's behind the plate calling strikes? It all matters.

03

Find the edge

The model predicts strikeouts, converts to probabilities, and compares to the sportsbook line. When the gap is big enough — that's the play.

The sharper the edge, the better the bet

Bigger disagreements between the model and the market mean bigger win rates.

What's "edge"?

The gap between what the model thinks will happen and what the sportsbook is pricing. A 5% edge means the model sees a significantly better chance of hitting than the odds imply. Over thousands of picks, that gap compounds into real profit.
