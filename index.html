How It Works

A transparent, data-driven approach to MLB player props.

1

Ingest Every Pitch

Every pitch from every game flows through our pipeline — velocity, movement, outcomes. Hundreds of thousands of data points per season.

2

Model the Matchup

Statistical models project expected stat lines for every starter, calibrated against years of out-of-sample data.

3

Find the Edge

We compute the true probability of going over or under each prop line — only bets that clear a strict confidence threshold make the cut.

4

Shop the Best Line

Every pick compares odds across 10+ sportsbooks and surfaces the best available price. Where you bet matters as much as what you bet.
